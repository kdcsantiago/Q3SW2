Si Janus Silang at ang Tiyanak ng Tabon is a book about a teenage boy named Janus who plays online games and discovers a secret about mythical creatures in his town. As he investigates, he uncovers a dangerous truth that threatens his family and friends. Title: Si Janus Silang at ang Tiyanak ng Tabon, Author: Edgar Calabia Samar, Date Published: January 1, 2014, Publisher: Adarna House
Florante at Laura is a popular poem in the Philippines. It's about a guy named Florante who goes through a lot of tough stuff, like love and war, while trying to find his girlfriend Laura. It's a really important poem that Filipino students learn about. Title: Florante at Laura , Author: Francisco Balagtas, Date Published: 1838, Publisher: C&E Publishing, Incorporated, 2002
Ibong Adarna is a famous Filipino folktale about a magical bird that can heal people. Three brothers try to catch the bird to heal their dad, but the bird's singing makes people fall asleep. It's a really old story that Filipino people have been telling for a long time. Title: Ibong Adarna, Author: José de la Cruz (Huseng Sisiw), Date Published: October 1941, Publisher: Prime Multi-Quality Printing Corporation, 2009
Noli Me Tangere is a famous novel in the Philippines written by Jose Rizal. The story is about a guy named Crisostomo Ibarra who comes back to the Philippines after studying in Europe. The book talks about how unfair and corrupt things were during the time when the Spanish were in charge of the country. It's an important book that has inspired people to fight for change. Title: Noli Me Tángere, Author: José Rizal, Date Published: 1887, Publisher: Berliner buchdruckerei-actien-gesellschaft
Mahabang-Mahabang-Mahaba is a short story written by Genaro R. Gojo Cruz. This short story talks about a boy who really hates his really long name. Later in the story, he finds out that his name is a combination of a lot of national heroes. He then started to appreciate his really long name. Title: Mahabang-mahabang-mahaba, Author: Genaro R. Gojo Cruz, Date Published: 2010, Publisher: Adarna House (2010)
